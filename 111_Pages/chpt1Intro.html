The Need for Structured Cabling Systems

Introduction

To understand the need for structured cabling systems, it is helpful to know what cabling systems consisted of in the past. Here you will learn about legacy and application-dependent cabling and why the change to structured cabling occurred. In the process, you will also learn about multi-conductor cables, coaxial cables and shielded twisted-pair cable that is still in use today. You will learn what systems in the Sound and Communication industry still rely on these cable types and become familiar with the purpose of their construction.

Objectives

  • Describe multi-conductor, coaxial, twinaxial and shielded twisted-pair cables
  • Identify systems in the Sound and Communication industry that use multi-conductor and coaxial cables
  • Describe the construction of multi-conductor and coaxial cables
  • Identify connectors used for coaxial cables
